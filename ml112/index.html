KNOW About
ML 112

This course has been designed keeping in mind the latest advancements in Machine Learning. The course focuses on giving an overview of Machine Learning to Undergraduate students at an intermediary level and majorly involves Mathematics, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning.
ML112 is free and open to all the students and will start on 22nd August 2022 from 07:00 PM IST. ML112 also includes 'DSE-A' and 'DSE-B of' the third semester of GGSIP University's BCA syllabus. 😊

DOWNLOAD SYLLABUS      

ROADMAP

  • Week 1

    Introduction & Applications

    In this week, you will be getting ready to learn about the applications and scope of Machine Learning with some basic algorithms.

  • Week 2

    Implementing Algorithms

    Week second includes learning some advanced algorithms and running over real-world data.

  • Week 3

    'Easy' Maths & Deep Learning

    By the end of this week, you will have a good understanding of essential calculus, Linear Algebra, and Deep Learning.

  • Week 4

    Wrap-up & Projects

    You will be working on the Major project and wrap-up for the course.

For these four weeks, you will be doing

Programming with Python 70%
Maths 5%
Projects 15%
Tests & Mock Interviews 10%
